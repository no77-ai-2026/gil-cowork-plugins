# EN 팩 — 소리블록 전 블록 드릴 (교재 기반)

> gil-education | language-tutor 참조 | `--mode drill/conversation/review` | 사용자 제공 교재 기반(개인 사용)

`en-soundblock.md`(블록 인덱스·훈련법)의 실습편 — **전 블록 그룹 커버리지**. 블록마다 시그니처 구문 + 샘플 드릴(순한맛 빈칸 / 매운맛 한→영 / 답안). 답안 발음은 소리튠 IPA 대조표로 자가점검. 튜터는 각 블록을 이 패턴으로 무제한 생성한다(샘플은 시드).

> 형식: **블록** — 시그니처 구문 / 매운맛 예: (한국어) → **답안** (영어) /IPA 핵심/

## A. be동사 시작블록
- **be part1** — I'm from · on my way · in the middle of · about to / 회의 중이라 통화 못 해 → I'm **in the middle of** a meeting /ɪn ðə ˈmɪdəl əv/
- **be part2** — I'm good at · ready for · glad to · here for / 도와드리려고 왔어요 → I'm **here for** support
- **be part3** — I'm afraid that · sorry for · sure of · nervous about / 늦을 것 같아요 → I'm **afraid that** I'll be late
- **be part4** — Be able to · likely to · aware of · prone to / 참석할 수 있을 거예요 → I'll **be able to** attend

## B. It's / There
- **It's 1** — It's just · time to · no wonder that · like / 이제 갈 시간이야 → It's **time to** go
- **It's 2** — Is it possible to · It is important to · It's easy to · Is it true / 변경 가능할까요? → **Is it possible to** change it?
- **There** — There is / There are / 문제가 하나 있어요 → **There is** a problem

## C. be ~ving / be pp
- **be ving1** — I'm getting · just trying to · thinking of · talking about / 그만둘까 생각 중이야 → I'm **thinking of** quitting
- **be ving2** — I'm dying to · telling · looking forward to · I was wondering if / 만나길 고대하고 있어요 → I'm **looking forward to** meeting you
- **be pp1** — surprised by · excited about · disappointed with/in · bored with · frightened / 결과에 실망했어요 → I'm **disappointed with** the result
- **be pp2** — satisfied with · worried about · used to · supposed to / 5시에 끝내야 해요 → I'm **supposed to** finish by 5

## D. 의지·요청 시작블록
- **wanna** — I wanna · don't wanna · Do you wanna / 점심 먹고 싶어 → I **wanna** grab lunch
- **want you to** — I want you to · don't want you to · Do you want me to / 이거 검토해줬으면 해 → I **want you to** review this
- **just wanted to** — I just wanted to · told you to · had no idea · didn't mean to / 그냥 인사하려고 했어 → I **just wanted to** say hi
- **like** — I like · don't like · I like it when · I like the way / 네가 설명하는 방식이 좋아 → I **like the way** you explain
- **think** — I think · don't think · Do you think · Don't you think / 그게 맞다고 생각 안 해 → I **don't think** that's right
- **I've got** — I've got · don't have · Do you have / 좋은 생각이 있어 → I've **got** an idea
- **have to** — have to · need · must · got to · don't have to · had to / 지금 가야 해 → I **have to** go now
- **know** — I know · Do you know · I don't know / 어떻게 하는지 알아 → I **know** how to do it

## E. 의문·제안·조동사
- **Why don't** — 좀 쉬는 게 어때? → **Why don't** you take a break?
- **what** — 무슨 말인지 모르겠어 → I don't know **what** you mean
- **how** — 이거 어떻게 작동해? → **How** does this work?
- **Can I** — 질문 하나 해도 될까요? → **Can I** ask a question?
- **Could** — 도와주실 수 있나요? → **Could** you help me?
- **may/might** — 늦을 수도 있어요 → I **might** be late
- **should** — 지금 떠나야 할까요? → **Should** we leave now?
- **would / would like to** — 커피 한 잔 하고 싶어요 → I **would like to** have a coffee

## F. 코어블록 (기본동사)
- **have** — 회의가 있어요 → I **have** a meeting
- **make** — 결정을 내려야 해 → I need to **make** a decision
- **get** — 메시지 받았어요 → I **got** your message
- **take** — 좀 쉴게요 → I'll **take** a break
- **do** — 할 수 있어요 → I can **do** it
- **go** — 집에 가자 → Let's **go** home
- **keep** — 계속 연락해요 → Let's **keep** in touch

## G. 디테일블록 (정보 확장)
- **장소(Location)** — at the office · in Seoul · near the station
- **빈도(Frequency)** — always · usually · sometimes · rarely
- **시간(Time)** — at 3 · by Friday · in the morning · for two hours
- 방법·목적·조건·동행·기간을 핵심 블록 뒤에 붙여 문장 확장.

## 튜터 사용
- `--mode drill`: 블록 선택 → 순한맛(빈칸 3) → 매운맛(한→영 3) → 답안(영어+IPA+한국어).
- `--mode conversation`: 블록을 상황 대화에 적용.
- `--mode review`: 약한 블록 누적 복습 + 쉐도잉(외부 음원) 안내.
