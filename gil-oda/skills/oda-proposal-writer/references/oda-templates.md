# ODA 사업 양식 모음

> gil-oda v0.1.0 | KOICA·EDCF·KSP·UN·WB·ADB 양식

## 개요

## 2. Log Frame (LFA) 매트릭스

### 표준 4x4

```
[Log Frame Matrix]

| Level | Narrative Summary | Objectively Verifiable Indicators (OVI) | Means of Verification (MOV) | Important Assumptions |
|-------|------------------|------------------------------------------|---------------------------|--------------------|
| Goal | 상위 목표 | OECD/UN 지표 | 통계청 | 정치 안정 |

## 3. KOICA Project Proposal (사업 제안서)

```
[KOICA Project Proposal]

1. Project Overview (1 page)
   - Title (KR + EN)
   - Period
   - Budget
   - Counterpart

2. Background and Rationale (3~5 pages)
   - Sector context
   - Development gap
   - Korean experience relevance
   - Korean ODA policy alignment
   - SDG mapping

3. Project Objectives (3 pages)
   - Goal
   - Purpose
   - Outputs

4. Project Components (5~10 pages)
   - Output 1: ...
     - Activities
     - Inputs
     - Schedule
   - Output 2: ...
   - ...

5. Implementation Arrangements (3 pages)
   - Korean side (KOICA + PMC)
   - Uzbek side (PMU + Counterpart)
   - Steering Committee

6. Budget (3 pages)
   - Korean contribution (KRW)
   - Uzbek matching (USD or in-kind)
   - Budget breakdown by activity

7. Implementation Schedule (1 page)
   - Gantt chart

8. Risk Analysis (3 pages)
   - Political, technical, environmental, social
   - Mitigation strategy

9. Monitoring & Evaluation (3 pages)
   - PDM
   - Reporting schedule

10. Expected Impact and Sustainability (3 pages)

11. Annexes
    - PDM
    - MOU
    - CVs of key personnel
    - Budget detail (Excel)
```

- Sector

- Counterpart (USD)

- Tied/Partially Untied/Untied

Section 2: Project Description
- Background
- Objectives
- Components
- Beneficiaries

Section 3: Preliminary Feasibility
- Technical (brief)
- Economic (brief CBA)
- Financial (brief)
- Environmental & Social (screening)

Section 4: Loan Terms (proposed)
- Interest rate (0.01~1.5%)
- Grace period (7~10 yrs)
- Repayment (25~40 yrs)

Section 5: Korean Industry Participation
- Tied/Untied
- Korean expertise
- Korean SMEs participation

Section 6: Implementation Schedule
Section 7: Budget Outline
Section 8: Risk Considerations
Section 9: F/S Recommendation
```

## 5. KSP Policy Consulting Proposal

```
[KSP Proposal]

1. Topic
   - Specific policy area
   - Korean experience to be shared

2. Background
   - UZ policy context
   - Need for Korean experience

3. Objectives
   - Policy report (1~3)
   - Workshops (3~5)
   - HLPC (1~2)
   - UZ policy adoption goal

4. Korean Consulting Team
   - Lead: KDI Director or Senior Researcher
   - Members: 5~10 (academia, policymakers)

5. Uzbek Counterpart
   - Lead: Vice Minister or higher
   - Members: Director General level

6. Activities (Quarterly)
   Q1: Identification, design, kick-off
   Q2~Q3: Field visits, workshops, reports
   Q4: HLPC, recommendations

7. Deliverables
   - Policy reports (EN + RU + UZ)
   - Workshop materials
   - Recommendations

8. Budget (1~3 KRW 100M)
   - Consultancy fees
   - Travel, accommodation
   - Workshop operations
   - Translation, publication

9. Expected Impact
   - UZ policy adoption
   - Korea-UZ cooperation strengthening
   - Follow-up KOICA/EDCF projects
```
